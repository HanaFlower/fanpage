Who is BTS?

BTS also known as Bangtan Boys is an acronym for the Korean expression Bangtan Sonyeondan which means Bullet Proof Boy Scouts. They are also known as Beyond the Scene for international fans. BTS is a seven member boy group which consists of the Leader RM, Jin, Suga, JHope, Jimin, V, and Jungkook. They debuted On June 13, 2013 under Bit Hit Entertainment with lead single "No More Dream" on their ablum " 2 Cool 4 Skool".They have a fan base known as A.R.M.Y which is also an acronym for Adorable Representative MC for Youth. Thier fan base is beyond unbelievable. BTS is the hottest Kpop act that is taking the world by storm!
